Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you use Zyrex Editing, we may collect:

  • Discord Account Information: When you log in via Discord OAuth, we receive your Discord ID, username, and avatar.
  • Verification Data: When you verify, we collect your IP address and country for security purposes.
  • Usage Data: Download counts and interactions are stored to improve our services.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To verify your identity for Discord server access
  • To detect and prevent abuse (alt account detection)
  • To improve user experience

3. Data Storage

Verification logs and download counts are stored on our secure servers. Discord account data is stored in session cookies only and is not permanently retained.

4. Cookies

We use essential session cookies for authentication. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics cookies beyond Cloudflare's standard services.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Discord: Authentication is handled via Discord OAuth. See Discord's Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare: Our site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may collect standard web traffic data. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense: We may display advertisements. See Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your verification data by contacting us via our Discord server. Session data is automatically cleared when you log out.

8. Contact

For privacy concerns, contact us via our Discord server.
